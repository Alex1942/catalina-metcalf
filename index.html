About Catalina

Where Mortgage Mastery Meets Luxury Real Estate

"I spent over 12 years evaluating real estate deals from the lender's side. Now I put that same precision and insight to work for my clients — so nothing surprises us at the closing table."

Catalina Metcalf brings a perspective to luxury real estate that very few agents can offer: the trained eye of a seasoned DE/SAR Mortgage Underwriter. With more than 12 years underwriting FHA, VA, and conventional loans across Brentwood, Franklin, and Nashville, Catalina has personally evaluated hundreds of real estate transactions — she knows exactly what lenders look for, how deals are structured, and where transactions can go sideways.


Now with Parks Realty, she channels that rare financial depth into a white-glove real estate experience for luxury buyers and sellers. Whether you're navigating a complex purchase or positioning a high-value property for sale, Catalina's mortgage intelligence gives you a decisive edge in Middle Tennessee's competitive luxury market.
